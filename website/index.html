What it does

Explore a paper through its references.

Annotated citations

PDF citation markers become hoverable previews with title, authors, year, venue, and abstracts when metadata is available.

Follow PDFs in place

Click a citation to resolve and open the cited paper in the same viewer, with back and forward navigation for the trail.

Session exports

Save the exploration graph as a self-contained HTML page, an Obsidian vault, or an optional Semble collection.

Privacy at a glance

Local-first, no ads, no developer data server.

Paper Browser processes PDFs and citation metadata to provide its reader features. It does not sell data, run personalized advertising, or send extension data to a Paper Browser-operated server. Some URLs, citation text, and metadata queries are sent to third-party scholarly services only as needed to resolve papers and metadata.
